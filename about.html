About Abromex Consult

Abromex Consult is a private consultancy registered in Ghana, providing continuing education and training in Microsoft Office Technology as well as research and data services to its target customers.

Abromex Consult is a Certiport Authorized Testing Center (CATC) providing Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) certification programmes, equipping information workers, professionals and learners with knowledge, competency, proficiency and mastery of Microsoft Office Technologies in order to support, enhance and achieve excellence in their Microsoft Office productivity.

Our Mission

To provide certified learning solutions, continuing education and training, research and data services to academics and the workforce to enhance continuous professional development.

Our Vision

To be the leading training provider on continuing education and training (CET) on Microsoft office technology in Ghana and beyond.

Values

  • Integrity
  • Honesty
  • Professionalism
  • Respect

Consultant Profile

Steven Bonsoe

Principal Consultant, Abromex Consult.

Steven Bonsoe is the Principal Consultant of Abromex Consult, a private consultancy providing continuing education and training (CET) in Microsoft Office Technology, professional spreadsheet consulting, financial modelling consulting, research and data analytic services.

Mr. Bonsoe holds a Bachelor of Business Administration from the University of Professional Studies, Accra (UPSA). He possesses Certificates in Population Survey Research Methods from the Institute of Statistical, Social and Economic Research (ISSER); Research Data Management and Sharing from the University of Carolina (USA) and University of Edinburgh (UK); PivotTables from the Excel University (USA); Excel Certificate from Corporate Finance Institute (USA) and Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Access (2010, 2013, 2016) from the Goodwill Community Foundation (Member, International Association of Continuing Education and Training – IACET).

Steven Bonsoe is one of the Microsoft Office Specialist Masters (MOSM) in Ghana and the one with a Microsoft Office Specialist Master (MOSM) in Ghana for Microsoft Office 2016, which is the most current version of the Microsoft Office Suite developed by Microsoft. He is a MOS Word Specialist and Expert, MOS Excel Specialist and Expert, MOS PowerPoint Specialist and MOS Access Specialist.

He is also a Financial Modelling Consultant, Spreadsheet Modeler & Programmer, Business Researcher, Data Analyst, Instructional Design Expert and CATC Consultant.
