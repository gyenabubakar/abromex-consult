MOS Training and Testing

Abromex Consult is a private consultancy registered in Ghana, providing continuing education and training in Microsoft Office Technology as well as research and data services to its target customers.

Research and Data Analytics Services

Our key strengths and expertise are in the areas of customer and employee satisfaction research; internet research; descriptive research and exploratory research.

We have expertise in the using Survey Monkey and Microsoft Technology for data collection. We use leading statistical packages such as SPSS, STATA and Microsoft Excel for data management, analysis and visualisation.

Financial Modeling Consultancy

We provide consulting services to key personnel and executives to help them develop operational financial models for their companies. We have expertise in developing financial models that do financial statements forecasting and company valuation.

Our main financial modelling tool is Microsoft Excel, which is the industry standard for financial modelling, forecasting and analysis.

Spreadsheet Consulting Services

We provide spreadsheet consulting services to all users of spreadsheets. Our spreadsheet consulting services aim at providing clients with knowledge, competency, proficiency and mastery of spreadsheets, especially Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets.

Our spreadsheet services include professional spreadsheet audit; spreadsheet model development; development of dashboards and reports; and spreadsheet programming.

Business Advisory Services

We provide business advisory services to small-and-medium sized enterprises (SMEs) in order to help them achieve higher levels of profitability and growth. Our business advisory services focus on improving the functional areas of business and the efficient deployment of underutilised information technology tools like Microsoft Office Technology.

This includes business formation and registration services; business and strategic planning; Information Technology Deployment; Microsoft Office Technology Deployment, Database Development, Administration and Maintenance.
